3D Character Model - Liar Larry

12/03/2025

A while back I had the opportunity to create a high quality character asset based on an existing piece of concept art for a university Module.

The character I picked to create in 3D was "Liar Larry" from a card game called "Gang Up!", and illustrated by Robin Keijzer. I chose this character because his pose and demeanour really stood out to me.

Final Character Model
The final model uses a combination of techniques to create a faithful and stylised recreation of the original art, I have done my best to captivate his charm in the animations for this character.

I modelled the character in Blender, using a subdivided low-poly base mesh. For texturing I used Substance Painter, implementing a combination of generators to generate edge outlines and ambient shadows, as well as hand painted details.
